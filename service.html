ICU

The Intensive Care Unit (ICU) at Chitwan Medical College is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to provide comprehensive care to critically ill patients. Our ICU is staffed with experienced healthcare professionals who are dedicated to providing the highest level of medical care.

  • 24/7 monitoring and care
  • Advanced life support systems
  • Specialized treatments for severe illnesses
  • Highly trained medical staff

NICU

The Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) is designed to care for premature and critically ill newborns. Our NICU team is committed to providing the best possible care to ensure the health and well-being of your baby.

  • Incubators and ventilators for premature babies
  • Specialized neonatal care
  • 24/7 monitoring by neonatal specialists
  • Family-centered care approach

OPD

The Outpatient Department (OPD) offers a wide range of services to diagnose and treat various medical conditions. Our OPD is designed to provide patients with easy access to medical consultations and treatments.

  • General medical consultations
  • Specialist consultations
  • Diagnostic services
  • Minor surgical procedures

USG

Our Ultrasound (USG) department is equipped with the latest technology to provide accurate imaging and diagnosis. We offer a range of ultrasound services to meet the needs of our patients.

  • Abdominal ultrasound
  • Obstetric ultrasound
  • Cardiac ultrasound
  • Doppler studies

CT Scan

The CT Scan department at Chitwan Medical College provides advanced imaging services to help diagnose a variety of conditions. Our CT scanners are capable of producing high-resolution images for accurate diagnosis.

  • High-resolution imaging
  • Non-invasive diagnostic tool
  • Quick and efficient scanning
  • Experienced radiologists

MRI

Our MRI department offers comprehensive imaging services using state-of-the-art MRI machines. MRI is a powerful tool for diagnosing a wide range of conditions, from brain and spinal cord abnormalities to joint and soft tissue injuries.

  • Advanced MRI technology
  • Detailed imaging for accurate diagnosis
  • Comfortable and safe scanning environment
  • Expert radiology team
